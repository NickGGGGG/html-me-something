Kansas City Trip!

So this is my girlfriend and I first trip out of town alone. We have been on one out of town trip before, and that was camping with her family. We are both 19 so finding a hotel was a little bit tricky, because most hotels you have to be 21 to check in:/ but luckly the Hotel-Motel-Holiday-Inn is not like that. You only have to be 18 to check in. We choose Kansas City, beacuse we're both 19, in school, with part time jobs, so we we're ballin a budget! Kansas city is not to expensive and we went to the Country Club Plaza which is a lot of fun!

The Train

We deicided to take a train there, because neither of us wanted to drive and I have never been on a train before. We took the Amtrak from Washington to Union Station. Union Station gives you a old timey train station vibe, which is really cool.

Arriving to Kansas City

As we arrived to Kansas city we got picked by a shuttle bus, courtesy of the Hotel-Motel-Holiday-Inn . The shuttle was able to take us anywhere around the hotel and luckly are hotel was close to Union Station and walking distance from the plaza. When we got checked in it our hotel, we dropped our bags off and made dinner reservation, but we still had an hour or two before then so we walked around the plaza. It was beauiful, the weather was a little windy on the first day, but still sunny.

FOOD

Being a human requires you to eat to survive, luckly my girlfriend and I love food, especically sushi. There was only one day that we didn't get sushi during our trip. At our hotel we got a free buffet breakfast. The best option at the buffet were the potato bites, They had some really seasoning. Besides breakfast and one night of room service, we ate at restruants in the Plaza and all of them were delicious!
